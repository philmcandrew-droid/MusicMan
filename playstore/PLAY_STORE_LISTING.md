## App Name
MusicMan - Musician Assistant

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Guitar tuner, chord libraries, idea capture, song builder & more for musicians.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
MusicMan is your all-in-one musician assistant — a comprehensive toolkit designed for guitarists, pianists, and songwriters of all levels.

**Guitar Tuner**
Tune your guitar with precision using real-time pitch detection. Tap any string to hear a reference tone. Supports standard tuning with visual feedback showing how sharp or flat you are.

**Guitar Chord Library**
Browse an extensive library of guitar chords across all 12 keys. Filter by root note and chord quality (major, minor, 7th, maj7, min7, sus4, power, jazz, and more). View CAGED system positions and inversions for every chord.

**Open Tunings Library**
Explore chord shapes for popular alternate tunings including Open D, Open G, Open E, Open A, Open C, DADGAD, and Drop D.

**Piano Chord Library & Builder**
Find piano chord voicings or build your own custom chords with the interactive chord builder.

**Idea Capture Studio**
Record musical ideas on the fly with a retro tape-deck interface. Features include:
• Voice memo recording with instant playback
• Real-time reverb and delay effects with adjustable knobs
• 3-band EQ (Bass, Mid, Treble) for shaping your sound
• Search and manage your saved recordings
• Notes/lyrics attached to each idea

**Song Structure Planner**
Plan and visualise your songs with an intuitive structure builder. Arrange verses, choruses, bridges, and more.

**Circle of Fifths**
Understand key relationships with an interactive Circle of Fifths diagram — essential for music theory and songwriting.

**AI Coach**
Get AI-powered feedback and guidance to improve your playing and writing.

**Additional Features**
• Dark and light mode
• Customisable tuner settings (sensitivity, reference frequency)
• Audio input/output device selection
• Left-handed mode for chord diagrams
• All data saved locally on your device

Whether you're practising at home, jamming with friends, or capturing inspiration on the go, MusicMan has everything you need in one app.
