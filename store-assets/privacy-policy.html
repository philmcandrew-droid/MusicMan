MusicMan Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 May 2026

Phil McAndrew ("we", "our", "us") built the MusicMan app as a free application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

MusicMan is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • Audio recordings: When you use the voice memo feature, audio is recorded using your device's microphone. All recordings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.
  • Text notes: Any text ideas you save are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. They are never sent to external servers.
  • Microphone access: The app requests microphone permission solely for the voice memo recording feature. Microphone data is only captured when you explicitly tap the Record button.

Data Storage

All user data (text notes, voice recordings, preferences) is stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to cloud services, analytics platforms, or third-party servers. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

AI Coach Feature

If you use the AI Coach feature, your prompts are sent to our backend server for processing. We do not store these conversations beyond the duration of the session. No personal information is required to use this feature.

Children's Privacy

MusicMan does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal data to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact
